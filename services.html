Direct tax services

Income tax and corporate tax advisory, planning and compliance support.

  • Representation before tax authorities and appellate forums
  • Income tax return filing and advance tax estimation
  • Tax planning advisory

Indirect tax services (GST)

Implementation, advisory and representation support on GST matters.

  • Assessment and representation support
  • Periodic return preparation and filing
  • Implementation and advisory services

International taxation

Cross-border tax support under Indian law and treaty positions.

  • DTAA-related taxation advisory
  • Transfer pricing assignments
  • 15CA / 15CB certifications and filings

Payroll services

Operational payroll support with employee tax advisory overlays.

  • End-to-end payroll handling
  • Payroll documentation and taxation advisory
  • Employee tax and investment sessions

Audit & assurance

Independent review, governance and statutory support.

  • Statutory audits under the Companies Act
  • Audit under the Income Tax Act
  • Internal audit and governance control checks
  • Concurrent audit engagements

Management advisory

Control frameworks, SOPs and process improvement engagements.

  • Management audits
  • SOPs, accounting manuals and control frameworks
  • Risk assessment and advisory
  • Business process re-engineering

Forensic auditing & fraud prevention

Investigative review and risk response support.

  • Investigation of embezzlement and fund misappropriation
  • Money laundering matters
  • Procurement fraud examination
  • Verification of customers, fixed assets and intermediaries

Transaction advisory services

Support for diligence, financing, structuring and deal-related work.

  • Liaison with banks and institutional investors
  • Legal, financial and commercial due diligence
  • M&A assistance
  • Project reports and agreement drafting/vetting
